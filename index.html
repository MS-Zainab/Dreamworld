About Dream World International

Founded in 2008, Dream World International has grown to become one of Lahore's most trusted travel agencies specializing in Hajj and Umrah.

Our mission is to deliver spiritually uplifting journeys with seamless logistics, comfortable accommodations, and expert guidance.

  • 15+ Years of experience in Hajj & Umrah services
  • 5000+ satisfied pilgrims served
  • Certified by Ministry of Hajj, Saudi Arabia
  • Member of Pakistan Association of Tour Operators (PATO)

Our office in Lahore's Purna Khana Nau area serves as the hub for our nationwide operations across Pakistan.
